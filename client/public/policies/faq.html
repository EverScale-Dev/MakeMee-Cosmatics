Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Are your skincare products safe to use?

Yes, our products are made with carefully selected ingredients and are safe for regular use when used as directed.

2. Are your products suitable for all skin types?

Our products are formulated to suit most skin types. Individual results may vary, so we recommend checking the product details before use.

3. Are your products vegan?

Yes, all our products are 100% vegan and made without any animal-derived ingredients.

4. How do I know which product is right for my skin?

Each product page includes usage instructions and recommendations to help you choose the right product.

5. Can I use your products if I have sensitive skin?

If you have sensitive skin, we recommend performing a patch test before full application. Discontinue use if irritation occurs.

6. How long does it take to see results?

Results vary depending on skin type and consistent usage. Visible improvements are typically seen over time.

7. How should I store the products?

Store products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep containers tightly closed after use.

8. What is the shelf life of your products?

Shelf life and expiry details are clearly mentioned on the product packaging.

9. Are your products safe to use during pregnancy?

We recommend consulting a healthcare professional before using skincare products during pregnancy.

10. How can I track my order?

You can track your order using the “My Orders” tab available on our website once it is shipped.

11. How can I contact customer support?

For any questions or assistance, please contact us at makemeecosmetics@gmail.com.
